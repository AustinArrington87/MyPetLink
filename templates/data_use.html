Data Use Agreement

Last updated: March 11, 2025

1. Agreement to Terms

By accessing and using MyPetLink's services, you agree to be bound by this Data Use Agreement. This agreement outlines how we handle and process your data, including pet health information, veterinary records, and personal information.

2. Data Collection and Processing

2.1 Types of Data

  • Pet health records and medical history
  • Veterinary visit information
  • Pet behavior and activity data
  • Location data for emergency services
  • User account information

2.2 Data Processing Purposes

  • Providing personalized pet health insights
  • Facilitating veterinary care coordination
  • Improving our services and user experience
  • Emergency response coordination
  • Research and development (anonymized data only)

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your data with:

  • Authorized veterinary professionals
  • Emergency services when necessary
  • Service providers who assist in operating our platform
  • Legal authorities when required by law

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. User Responsibilities

As a user, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate and complete information
  • Keep your account credentials secure
  • Update information when changes occur
  • Comply with all applicable laws and regulations

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide you services. You may request deletion of your data at any time, subject to legal requirements.

7. Research and Analytics

We may use anonymized and aggregated data for research and analytics purposes to improve our services and contribute to veterinary science. This data will not identify individual users or pets.

8. Third-Party Services

Our service may integrate with third-party services. These services have their own data use policies, and we encourage you to review them. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services.

9. Changes to Agreement

We may modify this agreement at any time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or through our platform. Continued use of our services after such modifications constitutes acceptance of the updated agreement.
